Prof. K. Rama Sastri, B.E., M.E., Ph.D.(IIT Madras), 1976 Dr. Rama Sastri was the Rector, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), until July 2002. Dr. Rama Sastri had held a wide variety of academic and administrative positions including Professor, Head of Department, Vice-Principal, Principal, Director and Registrar, JNTU prior to becoming the Rector of the University. He has published over 25 papers in national and international Journals and Conferences and he has guided more than 30 M.Tech. projects. He is also the honorary recipient of the Andhra Pradesh State Government “Best Teacher Award” for the University Teacher Category for the year 1994. Dr. Rama Sastri had been the Principal of the College since 2002. Under his dynamic leadership, CVR College has risen to a position of pre-eminence as an educational institution within just a few years, excelling in various engineering disciplines. Dr. Rama Sastri took charge as the Director in 2008.
